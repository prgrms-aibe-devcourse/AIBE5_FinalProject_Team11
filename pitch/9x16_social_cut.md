## Captions (mandatory for sound-off viewing)

- Position: **upper-third** (Reels/TikTok UI covers the bottom).
- Style: **white text, black outline 2 px, 44 pt, bold**.
- Use Descript's **karaoke / word-by-word highlight** style — proven to lift retention on Shorts.
- Burn in the captions (don't rely on platform auto-CC).
