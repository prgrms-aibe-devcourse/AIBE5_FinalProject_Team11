## Subtitle import (final step before export)

1. **Subtitles panel → Import** → choose `assets/teaser/teaser_ko.srt` for
   the Korean burn-in, **or** `teaser_en.srt` for English.
2. Position: bottom 12% margin. Style: white #FFFFFF, 36 pt, 70% black box,
   text-shadow 2 px.
3. For social cuts (9:16) move subs to the upper-third — see
   [9x16_social_cut.md](9x16_social_cut.md).
