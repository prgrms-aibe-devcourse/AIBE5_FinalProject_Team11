Implementing Technical Schema for Trust
Effective schema implementation is not merely about
SEO; it is about building explicit trust layers for AI.
Each piece of structured data acts as a signal, helping
generative engines understand the context, origin, and
reliability of your content.
This requires a meticulous approach to schema
markup, ensuring every relevant entity and relation-
ship is accurately described. Incomplete or incorrect
schema can degrade trust signals, potentially leading
to content being overlooked.
