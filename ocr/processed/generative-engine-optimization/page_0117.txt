5) Implementing a GEO Content Audit
Conduct a thorough audit of your existing content
to identify opportunities for Golden Answer creation.
Look for articles, FAQs, and product descriptions that
already contain clear, concise answers to common
questions. These can often be refactored into ideal
Golden Answer candidates.
Prioritize content that addresses high-volume, high-
intent queries where your brand offers a unique value
proposition. This strategic focus maximizes the impact
of your GEO efforts. The goal is to identify gaps where
your brand is not currently providing a definitive AI-
ready answer.
Develop a content roadmap specifically for GEO, out-
lining new Golden Answers to be created and existing
content to be optimized. This roadmap should align
with your overall content strategy but with a distinct
emphasis on generative retrieval and direct answer in-
jection. It becomes a living document, updated as AI
models evolve.
