Semantic Retrieval and Latent
Space Inclusion
Achieving inclusion in the latent space of LLMs de-
pends on optimizing for semantic retrieval. This
means your content must clearly communicate its core
concepts and relationships. LLMs interpret content not
just as words, but as vectors in a high-dimensional
space. The closer these vectors are to a user's query vec-
tor, the higher the retrieval probability.
