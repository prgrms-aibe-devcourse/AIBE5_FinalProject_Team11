Measuring SoMV is not a theoretical exercise; it
requires systematic execution. This involves rigor-
ous competitive analysis, benchmarking your brand's
citation frequency against key rivals for identical
prompts. The goal is to quantify your brand's dom-
inance and identify areas for strategic intervention
within the generative engine landscape.
Benchmarking Citation Frequency
Effective SoMV analysis begins with a comprehensive
prompt testing strategy. You must develop a diverse
set of prompts that accurately represent user queries
within your core product or service category. These
prompts should cover various intent types, including
informational, transactional, and comparative queries,
to capture a full spectrum of LLM responses.
Systematic testing involves submitting these prompts
to different LLM versions, including both current and
previous iterations, to observe citation patterns over
time. Document every instance where your brand or
a competitor's brand appears in the generated output.
