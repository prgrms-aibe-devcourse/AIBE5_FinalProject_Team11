1.3 SGE, Perplexity, and the New User Experience
The Search Generative Experience (SGE) from Google is
the most significant shift in search results pages in over
a decade. Instead of just a list of links, an "AI snapshot"
often appears at the top, providing a direct, synthesized
answer. Critically, these snapshots often include links
to the sources they used, displayed as small cards.
This is the new "Position Zero." Your goal in GEO is to
have your website featured in those source cards.
This changes user behavior dramatically. The need to
click multiple links is reduced. The user gets their an-
swer faster. If they do click, it's often on one of the
sources cited by the AI, which they now view with a
higher degree of trust. The game is no longer about get-
ting a click from the main list; it's about earning a cit-
ation from the AI.
