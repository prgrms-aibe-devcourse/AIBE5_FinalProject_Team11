8.3 Correlating GEO Efforts with Brand Mentions and Direct

Traffic

GEO success often manifests in ways that are harder to track directly.

Branded Search Volume: As you become a cited authority, more

people will search for your brand name directly. Monitor this trend

in Google Search Console. Direct Traffic: An increase in users typing

your URL directly into their browser is a strong sign of grow-

ing brand equity, often fueled by being seen in AI answers. Refer-

ral Traffic from Unexpected Sources: You may see traffic from AI-

powered apps or services that use your content as a source.

The goal is to show a correlation: "We implemented our GEO strat-

egy in Q2, and in Q3 we saw a 20% lift in branded search anda 15%

increase in direct traffic."
