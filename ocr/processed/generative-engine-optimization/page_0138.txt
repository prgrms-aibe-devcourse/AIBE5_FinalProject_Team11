Focus on creating comprehensive, expert-level content
that directly addresses specific long-tail queries. Gen-
erative models favor content that provides clear, con-
cise answers without excessive jargon or fluff. Struc-
tured data, such as FAQs and instructional guides, per-
forms particularly well.
Another key strategy involves establishing clear sub-
ject matter expertise. LLMs learn from the vast corpus
of the internet, and content consistently recognized as
expert in a given field will naturally gain higher cit-
ation weight. This means a sustained effort in thought
leadership.
Finally, monitor your SoMV regularly to identify areas
for improvement. If your SoMV declines for a specific
topic, analyze the generative responses to understand
competitor strategies. Adapt your content to fill infor-
mational gaps or present a more compelling narrative.
