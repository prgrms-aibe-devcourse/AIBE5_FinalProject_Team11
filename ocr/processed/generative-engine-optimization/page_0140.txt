Identifying Citation Gaps
Once you establish baseline SOMV metrics, the next
step involves identifying and filling Citation Gaps.
These gaps represent instances where competitors re-
ceive citations for prompts where your brand should
ideally be mentioned. Analyzing these gaps reveals spe-
cific knowledge deficits or perceived weaknesses in the
LLM's understanding of your brand's relevance.
For example, if a prompt about "best enterprise CRM
software" consistently cites a competitor but not your
CRM solution, a clear citation gap exists. This indicates
the LLM either lacks sufficient training data connecting
your brand to that specific query or it prioritizes com-
petitor information for other reasons. Understanding
the why behind these omissions is critical.
