1.2 How Large Language Models (LLMs) "Think"
You don't need a Ph.D. in computer science to under-
stand the basics of LLMs. Let's demystify it. Imagine an
incredibly powerful intern who has been forced to read
the entire internet—every Wikipedia page, every blog
post, every news article, every book.
This intern doesn't "understand" in the human sense.
Instead, it has learned the statistical patterns of lan-
guage. It knows that the word "queen" is often followed
by "of England" or "Elizabeth." It knows that a list of in-
structions is usually formatted with numbers or bullet
points.
