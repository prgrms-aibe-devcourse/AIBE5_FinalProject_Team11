This architecture offers significant advantages over

The retrieval phase itself involves complex semantic
matching algorithms. These algorithms do not just
look for keyword density. They assess the conceptual
relevance and informational integrity of content. For
example, a document might not contain an exact key-
word match but still prove highly relevant based on its
embedded knowledge graph.
Content Prioritization in LLMs
LLMs prioritize high-density information nodes over
keyword-stuffed pages. This contrasts sharply with
older SEO paradigms. A page rich in structured data,
unique insights, and comprehensive explanations
holds more weight. This is because such content pro-
vides more valuable context for the generative process.
