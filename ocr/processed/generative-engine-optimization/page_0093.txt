Designing for LLM Ingestion
Effective GEO deployment at scale requires a funda-
mental reevaluation of content creation and organiza-
tion. AI engines, particularly large language models,
prefer 'chunkable' content that fits within standard
context windows, such as 128k tokens. Overly long or
unstructured content often exceeds these limits, lead-
ing to truncated processing and missed opportunities
for citation.
Implementing an Atomic Content design is the stra-
tegic imperative here. This methodology treats each
piece of information as an independent, self-contained
unit. Each unit answers a specific question or presents
a single fact. This contrasts sharply with traditional
long-form articles that combine multiple ideas.
