A crucial metric for tracking generative engine visibil-
ity is Share of Model Voice (SoMV). SoMV represents
your brand's proportion of all relevant citations and
mentions across targeted generative AI models. For ex-
ample, we track SoMV across platforms like ChatGPT
and Gemini, providing a holistic view of your brand's
generative presence.
This metric offers a more nuanced understanding than
simple mention counts. It normalizes your brand's visi-
bility against the total conversational output for spe-
cific topics. A higher SoMV directly indicates stronger
influence and authority within the generative AI eco-
system.
