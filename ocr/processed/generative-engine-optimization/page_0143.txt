Challenges in AI Attribution
The primary hurdle involves the opaque nature of AI
interactions. Unlike a direct organic search result, an
Al-generated answer does not always provide a click-
able link back to the source. This means users might
encounter a brand name, product, or service within an
AI response without ever leaving the chatbot environ-
ment.
Another major issue stems from the user's journey
after an AI citation. While some users might seek out
the cited brand, many do not. This makes traditional
last-click or first-click attribution models largely in-
effective. We need new strategies to bridge this data gap
and demonstrate value.
