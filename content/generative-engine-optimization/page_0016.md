Implicit queries are the questions a user has but doesn't
| type. They are the natural follow-ups. If a user searches

"how to start an SEO campaign," their implicit queries

are:

"..for a small business?"

",..on a tight budget?"

",..how long does it take to see results?"

",..what tools do I need?"

By creating dedicated sections in your content that an-

swer these implicit queries (e.g., an H2 titled "How

Long Does SEO Take to Show Results?"), you are directly

feeding the AI the exact information it needs to build a

complete answer.

4.3 The Role of FAQs and "People Also Ask" in GEO

Google's "People Also Ask" (PAA) boxes and the FAQ

sections on competitors' pages are a goldmine for GEO.

They are a direct look into the most common implicit

and follow-up queries that users have.

1. Harvest these questions: Gather all relevant PAA

questions for your target topic.

2.Answer them definitively: Create a comprehensive

FAQ section at the end of your article or weave the an-

swers naturally throughout your content.
