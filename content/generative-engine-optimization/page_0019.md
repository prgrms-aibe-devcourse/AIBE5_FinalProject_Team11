5.2 Site Architecture for AI Agents

An AI agent doesn't just read one page; it tries to under-

stand your entire website. A messy, disorganized site

is confusing. A logical site architecture helps AI under-

stand context and hierarchy.

Create Information Silos: Group related content to-

gether. For example, all your blog posts about "Con-

tent Marketing" should live under a single category

(e.g., yourdomain.com/blog/content-marketing/) and

link heavily to each other. This creates a topical hub of

expertise.

Use Breadcrumbs: Breadcrumb navigation (e.g., Home

> Blog > SEO > This Post) helps both users and AI agents

understand where they are in your site's hierarchy.

Strategic Internal Linking: Link from your high-au-

thority pages to new content you want to boost. Use

descriptive anchor text (e.g., link to your schema guide

with the text "learn more about schema markup") to

provide context.
