Aligning with Retrieval Score Logic
The concept of retrieval score is central to this optimiza-
tion. Generative engines assign a score to potential
sources based on various factors, including the clarity,
specificity, and directness of the information. Content
that directly answers a query with high confidence and
minimal ambiguity receives a higher score.
