To pinpoint these gaps, compare your brand's citation
| frequency with that of each competitor across all

tested prompts. Focus on discrepancies where a com-

petitor's citation rate significantly surpasses yours for

relevant queries. These are your primary targets for

intervention and optimization.

Strategic Intervention for

SoMV Improvement

Filling Citation Gaps requires a multifaceted approach,

blending traditional content optimization with gen-

erative-specific strategies. First, review your existing

digital footprint to ensure brand mentions

are clear, consistent, and contextually rich across all

owned and earned media. LLMs learn from the vast cor-

pus of internet data, so a strong, coherent online pres-

ence is foundational.

Next, consider targeted content creation specifically

designed to influence LLM training data. This might

involve publishing authoritative articles, detailed com-

parison guides, or case studies that explicitly link

your brand to specific solutions or use cases. Empha-

size unique selling propositions and differentiate your

offerings from competitors directly within this con-

tent.
