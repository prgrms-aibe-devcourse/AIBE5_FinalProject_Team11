5.4 The GEO Technical Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to audit your site's technical readiness for GEO:

HTTPS is enabled: The site is secure.

Mobile-Friendly: The site works perfectly on all devices.

Core Web Vitals Pass: Use Google PageSpeed Insights to test your

scores. Key Schema is Implemented: At minimum, Article, Organ-

ization, and Person schema should be present. Use FAQPage and

HowTo where relevant.

XML Sitemap is Clean and Submitted: Your sitemap tells crawlers

what pages to find.

No Major Crawl Errors: Check Google Search Console for any issues.

Logical Site Architecture: Content is organized into clear categor-

ies/silos. Internal Linking is Strategic: Important pages are well-

linked with contextual anchor text.
