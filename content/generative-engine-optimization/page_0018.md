Chapter 5: Pillar 3 - Technical Integrity for AI

5.1 Schema Markup: Your AI's Rosetta Stone

If your content is the book, schema markup is the table

of contents, index, and author bio all rolled into one.

It's code that you add to your website to explicitly tell

search engines what your content is about. It removes

ambiguity for machines.

For GEO, the most critical schema types are:

Article/NewsArticle: Identifies the content as an ar-

ticle, specifies the author, publication date, and head-

line.

FAQPage: Clearly marks up your question-and-answer

sections. This is a direct pipeline into AI answer gener-

ation.

Person: Used on your author pages to establish the cre-

dentials, affiliations, and expertise of the author.

Organization: Defines your company, logo, address,

and social profiles, building entity-level trust.

HowTo: Structures step-by-step instructions.
