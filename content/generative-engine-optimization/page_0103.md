5) Specific Linguistic Patterns for Citation
Certain linguistic patterns significantly increase the
probability of AI citations. Phrases like "The primary
cause is...", "Research shows that...", "Data indicates...",
or "According to [Source],..." act as strong signals for
LLMs. These patterns explicitly flag a statement as an
attributable fact, guiding the AI toward direct citation
rather than mere synthesis.
Content structured with these triggers provides expli-
cit cues for the AI's retrieval mechanisms. It's not just
about stating a fact, but presenting it in a manner that
clearly delineates it as a distinct, citable piece of infor-
mation. This strategic phrasing directly influences the
model's confidence in attributing the information to
your source.
