To consolidate these indirect signals, develop Direct-
to-Brand attribution models. These models prioritize
user actions that bypass traditional digital market-
ing funnels, such as direct site visits, branded organic
searches, or direct manual searches for specific offers.
The model accounts for the user's journey from AI ex-
posure to a direct brand interaction.
