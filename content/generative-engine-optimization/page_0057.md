Use clear, declarative sentences that present facts un-
| equivocally. Avoid ambiguity or overly promotional
language, which an AI may filter out as less objective.
The goal is to produce content that an AI can easily
parse and integrate as a factual statement, devoid of
subjective interpretations.
Structured data plays a critical role in enhancing con-
tent's citability. Implementing schema markup, par-
ticularly for facts, definitions, and entity relationships,
provides explicit signals to AI models about the nature
and context of your content. This makes it significantly
easier for an AI to extract and present your brand's in-
formation as an authoritative answer.
Consider the interconnectedness of your content. A
strong internal linking structure that reinforces key
topics and connects related expertise helps build a co-
herent brand graph. This internal network signals to AI
models the breadth and depth of your brand's know-
ledge domain, making it a more attractive citation
source.
