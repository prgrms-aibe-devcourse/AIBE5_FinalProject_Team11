Generative engines are being trained to identify and reward signals

of genuine expertise to avoid spreading misinformation. Anyone

can use an AI to write a generic article about finance. But only a real

financial expert can add personal stories of market volatility, unique

insights from their career, and verifiable credentials. That is what

GEO seeks to reward.
