My guiding philosophy for SEO, and now for GEO, is simple: Build
o Value, Not Illusions. Don't spend your time trying to create the il-
lusion of authority or quality. Spend your time creating the actual
thing. Write the best, most helpful guide. Conduct original research.
Share your hard-won experience. Build a website that is fast, clear,
and trustworthy. If you focus on creating genuine value for the user,
you will naturally align with the longterm goals of any search or an-
swer engine. That is the most sustainable strategy there is.
Chapter 10: The Next 5 Years in Search
10.1 The Rise of Autonomous AI Agents
The evolution doesn't stop with SGE. The next step
is autonomous AI agents. These will be proactive as-
sistants that don't just answer questions, but complete
tasks.
Instead of searching "best flights to Tokyo," you'll say,
"Book me the most costeffective flight to Tokyo for the
first week of June, on an airline I have status with,
with a layover under 2 hours, and book a hotel near the
Shinjuku station."
Optimizing for this future means having highly struc-
tured, machine-readable data about your products and
services (e.g., real-time flight availability, hotel room
features) that an AI agent can access and act upon via
APIs.
