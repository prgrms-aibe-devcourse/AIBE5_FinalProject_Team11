9.2 Transparency and AI Content Disclosure

The debate is ongoing about whether to explicitly label Al-assisted

content. My position is one of transparency. While you don't need to

put a giant banner on every post, being honest is good practice. In

the future, search engines may even reward it.

Consider a simple disclaimer in your site's footer or on your about

page: "Our content creation process leverages AI tools for ideation and

drafting, but all articles are heavily edited, fact-checked, and enriched

with unique insights by our team of human experts to ensure accuracy

and value." This builds trust.
