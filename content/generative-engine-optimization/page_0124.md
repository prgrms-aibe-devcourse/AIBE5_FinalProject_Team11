PDF Whitepaper Preparation
PDF whitepapers require specific optimization for LLM
ingestion. Avoid image-only PDFs; ensure all text is
selectable and searchable. Use clear headings, bullet
points, and concise paragraphs to break down complex
information.
Structure your whitepapers with a consistent logical
flow. This helps GPT-40 establish relationships be-
tween different sections and concepts. Embed metad-
ata where possible, including keywords and summar-
ies, to further guide the model's understanding.
Long, dense paragraphs increase the cognitive load on
the LLM. Break these down into smaller, digestible
chunks. This improves the chances of specific data
points being accurately extracted and cited during con-
versational interactions.
