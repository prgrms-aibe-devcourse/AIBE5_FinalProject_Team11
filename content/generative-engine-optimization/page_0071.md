Here are key schema types to prioritize for trust layer
| engineering:

> Person Schema: Clearly define authors, editors, and

reviewers with their credentials, affiliations, and veri-

fied online profiles. » Organization Schema: Detail your

organization's legal name, contact information, official

websites, and any awards or certifications.

> Article/NewsArticle Schema: Include publication

dates, modification dates, publisher information, and

references to cited works.

> WebPage Schema: Mark up privacy policies, terms of

service, and contact pages to signal transparency and

user-centric practices.

>FactCheck Schema: For content that verifies claims,

use this to explicitly signal factual accuracy and meth-

odology.

Regularly audit your schema implementation for ac-

curacy and completeness. Outdated or conflicting

schema can send mixed signals to AI engines, under-

mining your trust-building efforts. Consistent main-

tenance is crucial.
